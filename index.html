CGPA Calculator calculates the total CGPA of an individual using the universal calculation method. To calculate/estimate your CGPA, first input your total credit and CGPA earned till now. Then enter the current semester/course and the acquired GPA. Use “Add another course” button to add the GPA of other courses. The results of the total CGPA will be shown. If you plan to retake a course to improve the result of that particular course, the retake feature helps edit the course's previous data and input the recent data to provide an accurate estimate of the updated CGPA.
